About Us

Our Spiritual Journey

Pandit Subodh Pandey was founded with a divine mission to connect devotees with authentic, knowledgeable pandits for their religious ceremonies. With over 15 years of service in the spiritual domain, we understand the profound significance of these sacred occasions and ensure that every ritual is performed with utmost devotion, precision, and adherence to Vedic traditions.

Our team meticulously verifies each pandit's credentials, expertise, and experience through a rigorous selection process that includes background checks, assessment of Vedic knowledge, and evaluation of ceremonial execution skills. We serve all localities across Bangalore and specialize in a wide spectrum of ceremonies including weddings, griha pravesh, naamkaran, mundan, satyanarayan katha, havan, and daily poojas.

What sets us apart is our commitment to preserving the authenticity of Vedic rituals while adapting to the needs of modern households. Our pandits are not only well-versed in scriptures but also possess the ability to explain the significance of each ritual in simple terms, creating a more meaningful experience for all participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pandit booking services

How far in advance should I book a pandit?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for standard ceremonies. For weddings and major events, we suggest booking 1-2 months ahead to ensure availability of our most experienced pandits. However, we understand that some ceremonies need to be arranged urgently, and we try our best to accommodate last-minute requests based on pandit availability.

What areas of Bangalore do you serve?

We serve all areas of Bangalore including Central, North, South, East, and West Bangalore. Our pandits are located across the city to ensure timely arrival at your ceremony. We cover all major localities like Whitefield, Electronic City, Yelahanka, Rajarajeshwari Nagar, Koramangala, Indiranagar, Jayanagar, Malleshwaram, and many more. Please contact us if you need service in a specific area.

What is included in your service?

Our service includes a verified, experienced pandit who will perform the ceremony according to Vedic traditions. The pandit will bring all necessary puja materials (additional charges may apply for materials). Guidance throughout the ceremony is included. For certain ceremonies, we can also arrange for additional helpers, audio systems for mantras, and other requirements at an extra cost. Please specify your needs during booking.

How are your pandits verified?

All our pandits undergo a rigorous verification process including background checks, verification of qualifications and experience, and assessment of their knowledge of Vedic rituals. We also check customer feedback and ratings. Many of our pandits come from traditional priest families with generations of experience. We continuously monitor performance through customer feedback to maintain our high standards.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 48 hours before the ceremony without any charges. For cancellations within 48 hours, a nominal fee may apply depending on the circumstances. In case of emergencies, we try to be as flexible as possible. Rescheduling is subject to pandit availability. We recommend informing us as early as possible for any changes to your booking.

Do your pandits speak English and Kannada?

Yes, most of our pandits are fluent in English, Kannada, and Hindi. During the booking process, you can specify your language preference, and we will assign a pandit who can communicate in your preferred language. Our pandits are skilled at explaining rituals in simple terms regardless of the language, ensuring all participants understand and appreciate the ceremony.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing varies based on the type of ceremony, duration, complexity, and the experience level of the pandit. Basic ceremonies start from ₹1500, while more complex rituals like weddings may range from ₹5000 to ₹15000. The final cost will be communicated during the booking confirmation. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden charges.
